From October 2013 to December 2016, I have been a PhD student in the Condensed matter theory group of the Laboratoire de Physique Statistique (École Normale Supérieure, Paris), under the supervision of Werner Krauth. I am interested in the field of numerical simulations of condensed matter and cold atomic systems, and worked in particular on path-integral quantum Monte Carlo simulations for the unitary Bose gas.
