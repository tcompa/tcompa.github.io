--- layout: default title: Home --- Since October 2013, I am a PhD student in the Condensed matter theory group of the Laboratoire de Physique Statistique (École Normale Supérieure, Paris), under the supervision of Werner Krauth. I am interested in the field of numerical simulations of condensed matter and cold atomic systems, and in particular I am working on quantum Path Integral Monte Carlo simulations for the unitary Bose gas. This work is based on some recent research activity in our group, see:
S. Piatecki and W. Krauth, Efimov-driven phase transitions of the unitary Bose gas (arXiv, Nature Communications).
